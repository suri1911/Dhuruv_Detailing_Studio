Who We Are

Dhuruv Car Accessories & Detailing Studio

At Dhuruv Car Accessories & Detailing Studio, automotive care is elevated to an art form. We redefine excellence through precision detailing, refined craftsmanship, and uncompromising quality.

Every vehicle that enters our studio receives meticulous attention, premium-grade products, and advanced techniques designed to restore, protect, and enhance its true character. From elite paint protection and ceramic coatings to bespoke interior enhancements and curated car accessories, we deliver sophistication in every detail.

Our commitment is simple — perfection without compromise. We blend passion with innovation to ensure your vehicle reflects elegance, power, and prestige.

Our expert team combines precision, modern technology, and premium products to deliver a flawless finish every time. Whether it's restoring showroom shine, upgrading interiors, or protecting your vehicle's paint, we treat every car like our own.

Founder & CEO

Mr. Selvakumar

Founder & CEO — Dhuruv Car Accessories & Detailing Studio

Mr. Selvakumar is a Mechanical Engineering graduate with over 10 years of extensive experience in the automotive industry. Having worked with the globally recognized Hyundai brand for several years, he has gained deep technical expertise, industry insight, and a strong foundation in quality-driven automotive standards.

His professional journey reflects dedication, discipline, and a commitment to excellence. With a passion for precision and a vision to elevate automotive care, Mr. Selvakumar established Dhuruv Car Accessories & Detailing Studio to deliver premium detailing services and high-end car accessories that meet world-class standards.

Under his leadership, the studio embodies innovation, craftsmanship, and uncompromising quality — setting new benchmarks in automotive luxury care.
